Assignment - Cameras
Zzlore by Vrunal and Saurabh 

Controlling the player - Arrow keys 
Toggle between first person and third person - 'C' 
Throw energy balls if energy orb collected - left mouse button
Restart the level - 'R'

Goal - reach the end before you run out of time (time runs out if map collectible collected). 
     - Get as many of the purple blocks you can by energizing them with energy balls. ( throw energy balls at them) 
     
Cameras 
1. First person camera 
2. Third person camera 
3. Mini map camera follow 

Collectibles - 
1. Energy orb that gives you the ability to throw energy balls at purple blocks. 
2. Map Collectible (Yellow coin) - ability to view the a mini map of the labyrinth but it also starts the timer 
3. Time restriction - you get 60 seconds to reach the end. 

Some bugs -
Player tumbles over at times if the terrain is uneven ( restart the game in such cases) 
View of the outside world at times.
